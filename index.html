Privacy Policy

Hourly Rate Timer  |  Effective date: May 2026

✔ No data collected  ·  ✔ Works fully offline  ·  ✔ Nothing shared
Hourly Rate Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device only.

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Hourly Rate Timer. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Who We Are

Hourly Rate Timer is an independent Android application developed for freelancers, consultants, and anyone who bills clients by the hour. The app is available on the Google Play Store.

3. What Data We Collect

Hourly Rate Timer collects no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, the app does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any other identifying information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial information
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

All data you enter in the app — such as project names, session durations, and your hourly rate — is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

4. Internet Access

Hourly Rate Timer does not connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline. No data is sent to or received from any server, cloud service, or third party.

5. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your Android device using standard Android storage. This data includes:

  • Project and session names you have created
  • Recorded session durations
  • Your configured hourly rate and currency preference

This data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the application.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers involved in this application.

7. Exported Files

Hourly Rate Timer allows you to export session summaries as PDF or CSV files. These files are saved to your device's storage and are under your full control. We do not have access to any exported files.

8. Children's Privacy

Hourly Rate Timer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Since the app collects no data at all, it poses no privacy risk to any age group.

9. App Permissions

Hourly Rate Timer requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function. Any permission related to storage is used solely to allow you to save exported files to your own device.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be made available on the Google Play Store listing. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Google Play Store listing for Hourly Rate Timer.

This policy is effective as of May 2026.  ·  Hourly Rate Timer
